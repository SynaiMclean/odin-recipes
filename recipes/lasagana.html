Lasagana

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce (commonly a tomato-based ragù), creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella or Parmesan cheese. Baked until bubbly and golden, lasagna is hearty, flavorful, and comforting, with each bite offering a satisfying blend of savory sauce, tender pasta, and gooey cheese. Variations include vegetarian versions with spinach, mushrooms, or zucchini, and regional styles that adjust the ingredients or sauce to local tastes.

Ingredients

    For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

    • For Cheese Layer

  • 5 oz (425g) ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese as an alternative)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
